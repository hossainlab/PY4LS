Dataset:  beerhall.dat

Source: John Clay (1857). "On the Relation Between Crime, Popular
Instruction, Attendance on Religious Worship, and Beer-House",
Journal of the Statistical Society of London, Vol. 20 #1, pp 22-32.

Description: Variables for British counties circa 1850 for
crime, public bars, school attendance, and church attendance.

Variables/Columns
County  1-16
Region Name  18-36
Region code  43
Criminals per 100k population  48-51
Ale/Beer Houses per 100k      56-59
Attendants @ school per 10k   64-67
Attendants @ public worship per 2000  72-75
